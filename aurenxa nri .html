Got Questions?

Everything You
Need to Know

Common questions from NRI clients considering their first build or renovation in Bengaluru. Don't see yours? Reach out and we'll respond within 24 hours.

Ask Us Anything →

Do I need to visit Bengaluru during the project?

+

Not necessarily. Most of our NRI clients complete entire projects entirely remotely via video walkthroughs, digital approvals, and couriered documents. A visit is always welcome, but never required.

How do I send money for the project from abroad?

+

We accept NEFT, RTGS, and international wire transfers. All payments are invoiced and receipted. We guide you through FEMA-compliant fund transfers to ensure there are no legal complications.

How do BBMP approvals work for NRI-owned properties?

+

Our in-house legal team manages the entire BBMP plan sanction process on your behalf. We require a power of attorney from you — our team guides you on how to execute this correctly from abroad.

What does a Dedicated NRI Project Manager do?

+

One point-of-contact project manager who knows your project end-to-end. They adjust communication to your time zone, respond within 24 hours, and coordinate all internal teams on your behalf.

How are design decisions made remotely?

+

We share 3D renders, material boards, and finish samples digitally. Video calls for key design reviews, digital sign-offs at each milestone, and physical samples couriered to you when needed.

Do you manage the property after completion?

+

Yes. Our post-handover property management covers maintenance, tenant sourcing and coordination, utility management, and regular condition reports — ideal for NRIs not returning immediately.

Which areas of Bengaluru do you build in?

+

We operate across all of Bengaluru — Koramangala, Indiranagar, Whitefield, Sarjapur, JP Nagar, Jayanagar, Hebbal, and surrounding areas. We assess every site before committing to a project.
